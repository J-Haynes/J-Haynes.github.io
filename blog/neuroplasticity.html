Neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity is the concept that the human brain can modify its neural connections in response to new experiences, new ideas, and changes in environment. The brain is constantly looking to form new neural pathways, giving us the ability to learn new ideas. It is a relatively new idea that challenges the belief that the brain grows until a certain age and will remain unchanged onwards. The new model of a dynamic, ever changing brain, has transformed the way we think about learning, especially later in life.
